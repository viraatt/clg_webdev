Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingrediants

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups of bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark broun sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast , sugar , eggs
  2. Mix the milk with yeast , sugar , eggs
  3. Mix the milk with yeast , sugar , eggs
